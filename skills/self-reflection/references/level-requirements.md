# PM Level Requirements

Read ONLY the section for the PM's stated level. Requirements are cumulative (each level includes everything below it). The skill should map evidence to the PM's level requirements, not levels above.

---

## C4 (Apprentice Product Manager)

### Craft Skills
- Out-care everyone else with the problem you are set to solve.
- Do things when you said you will.

### Responsibilities To The Craft/Job
- Deeply understand customer needs and be the voice of your future customers.
- Constantly charge your trust battery and make your team and product better.
- Deliver on your assigned work.

### Responsibilities To Shopify
- Treat current product quality as a minimum floor. Be ambitious about raising the ceiling.

---

## C5 (Product Manager)

### Cumulative: All C4, plus:

### Craft Skills (new)
- Know all the important details.
- Define problems clearly, choose the ones worth solving.
- Clearly communicate about the product, and any directional changes.

### Responsibilities To The Craft/Job (new)
- Hold the quality bar sky-high.
- Take responsibility for the success and quality of your products.

### Responsibilities To Shopify (new)
- Inspire your team.

---

## C6 (Senior PM)

### Cumulative: All C5, plus:

### Craft Skills (new)
- Choose problems worth solving, fight for the right solution, influence decisions quickly.
- Be resourceful. Do more with less.

### Responsibilities To The Craft/Job (new)
- Drive cross functional, cross discipline stakeholder alignment.
- Exhibit good judgment. Be right often.

### Responsibilities To Shopify (new)
(No new Shopify responsibilities at C6.)

---

## C7 (Staff PM)

### Cumulative: All C6, plus:

### Craft Skills (new)
- Improve the way we build great products, teach by example.
- Be self reliant and do it yourself first.
- Operate independently, solving complex product problems.

### Responsibilities To The Craft/Job (new)
- Model accountability for the products you build; understand and share impact. Customer feedback is your barometer.
- Play a key role in solving the hardest parts of the hardest problems of your projects.
- Simplify so your team can go faster.

### Responsibilities To Shopify (new)
- Don't constrain yourself to existing limitations, unless they happen to be ideal.
- Inspire Shopify.

---

### Cumulative: All C7, plus:

### Craft Skills (new)
(No new craft skills at C8.)
