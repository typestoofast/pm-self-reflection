# PM Level Requirements

Read ONLY the section for the PM's stated level. Requirements are cumulative -- each level includes everything below it plus new additions.

Three categories per level:
1. **Craft Skills** -- How you practice the PM craft
2. **Responsibilities To The Craft/Job** -- What you're accountable for
3. **Responsibilities To Shopify** -- How you contribute to the company

---

## C4 (Apprentice Product Manager)

### Craft Skills
- **Out-care everyone else** with the problem you are set to solve
- **Do things when you said you will** -- reliability is foundational

### Responsibilities To The Craft/Job
- **Deeply understand customer needs** and be the voice of your future customers
- **Constantly charge your trust battery** and make your team and product better
- **Deliver on your assigned work**

### Responsibilities To Shopify
- **Treat current product quality as a minimum floor.** Be ambitious about raising the ceiling

### Evidence patterns at C4
Look for: customer research notes, delivery consistency, product quality improvements, trust-building moments with the team.

---

## C5 (Product Manager)

### Cumulative: All C4 requirements, plus nothing new

C5 has the same formal requirements as C4. The distinction is demonstrating these at higher complexity and with more independence. Evidence should show the PM handling more ambiguous problems and operating with less direction.

### Evidence patterns at C5
Same as C4, but with evidence of increased scope, ambiguity, and self-direction.

---

## C6 (Senior PM)

### Cumulative: All C4/C5, plus:

### Craft Skills (new)
- **Know all the important details** of your area
- **Define problems clearly**, choose the ones worth solving
- **Clearly communicate** about the product and any directional changes
- **Choose problems worth solving**, fight for the right solution, influence decisions quickly
- **Be resourceful.** Do more with less

### Responsibilities To The Craft/Job (new)
- **Hold the quality bar sky-high**
- **Take responsibility** for the success and quality of your products
- **Drive cross-functional, cross-discipline stakeholder alignment**
- **Exhibit good judgment.** Be right often

### Responsibilities To Shopify (new)
- **Inspire your team**

### Evidence patterns at C6
Look for: deep domain knowledge, clear problem definitions in docs, communication artifacts (strategy updates, directional changes), cross-functional alignment efforts, judgment calls that proved correct, quality bar enforcement.

---

## C7 (Staff PM)

### Cumulative: All C6, plus:

### Craft Skills (new)
- **Improve the way we build great products**, teach by example
- **Be self-reliant** and do it yourself first
- **Operate independently**, solving complex product problems

### Responsibilities To The Craft/Job (new)
- **Model accountability** for the products you build; understand and share impact. Customer feedback is your barometer
- **Play a key role** in solving the hardest parts of the hardest problems of your projects
- **Simplify so your team can go faster**

### Responsibilities To Shopify (new)
- **Don't constrain yourself** to existing limitations, unless they happen to be ideal
- **Inspire Shopify**

### Evidence patterns at C7
Look for: process improvements that helped the team build better, independent problem-solving on complex issues, accountability artifacts (impact sharing, customer feedback loops), simplification efforts, challenging the status quo, influence beyond the immediate team.

---

## C8 (Senior Staff PM)

### Cumulative: All C7, plus:

### Craft Skills (new)
(No new craft skills beyond C7)

### Responsibilities To The Craft/Job (new)
- **Pull products out of local maxima** -- push past incremental into transformative
- **Clearly articulate a multi-year mission and vision** for a product
- **Be active in attracting top talent** and help interview and assess candidates
- **Act as an escalation point** for critical projects in need of intervention

### Responsibilities To Shopify (new)
- **Conceive, iterate, and launch products** that make commerce better for everyone

### Evidence patterns at C8
Look for: product vision documents, multi-year roadmaps, talent attraction efforts, escalation handling, products that broke out of incremental improvement, new product launches.

---

## C9 (Principal PM)

### Cumulative: All C8, plus:

### Craft Skills (new)
- **Constantly move Shopify to new global maxima** that define world-class products
- **Improve the long-term health** of our products and customer experiences

### Responsibilities To The Craft/Job (new)
- **Rally Shopify** behind multi-year mission and vision
- **Drive material financial impact** for Shopify

### Responsibilities To Shopify (new)
- **Innovate through big bets** that can transform Shopify
- **Aim to solve problems several moves ahead**

### Evidence patterns at C9
Look for: company-wide rallying artifacts, financial impact quantification, big bet proposals, long-term health improvements, forward-looking strategy, global maxima pushes (not just local optimization).

---
